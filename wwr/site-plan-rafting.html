Overview

Purpose

Water Rafting is an adventurous sport that will bring even more happiness to someone's life. The purpose of the website is to increase our company online presence and reach a bigger number of new adventurous customers.

Audience

What are the target audience? The terget audience is young adventurous people 20-40 years old in age, middle to higher class interested in nature sports.

What do they want? They are searching for high adrenaline adventure to help them relax from common stressor of life.

What do they except to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website? The website will help to introduce them to the concept of Water Rafting, will present a schedule for availabilities and will quote prices for adventures.
