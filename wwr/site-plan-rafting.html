Overview

Purpose

Water Rafting is an adventurous sport that will bring even more happiness to someone's life. The purpose of the website is to increase our company online presence and reach a bigger number of new adventurous customers.

Audience

The aterget audience is young adventurous people 20-40 years old in age, middle to higher class interested in nature sports. The website will help them to plan their water rafting adventure and will show schedule availabilities, introduce them to the sport and show prices. The website will be accessible through any kind of devide: laptops, phones or tablets.
